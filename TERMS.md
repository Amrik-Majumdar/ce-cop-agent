# Terms Of Use

Last updated: July 2026

CE Cop Agent is a real estate lead-response workflow project. It is provided for educational, portfolio, prototype, and public review purposes unless separately deployed under written terms.

## Service Description

The project is designed to help respond to inbound real estate inquiries, ask basic qualification questions, and route useful context for human follow-up. It does not replace licensed real estate professionals, legal advice, brokerage compliance review, fair-housing compliance review, advertising compliance review, or the professional judgment of the team using it.

## User Responsibility

Users and deployers are responsible for:

- confirming that messages and workflows comply with brokerage rules
- reviewing lead handoffs and following up appropriately
- protecting private lead records and credentials
- honoring applicable real estate, advertising, privacy, communication, and fair-housing rules
- avoiding submission of unnecessary sensitive information

## Prototype Status

Public repository materials may show product structure, interface flow, and supporting documents. They do not guarantee a production service, live backend, uninterrupted availability, lead volume, appointments, clients, or closed transactions.
